# Dots and Boxes

## Overview

Classic pen-and-paper strategy game where players take turns drawing lines between dots, trying to complete boxes and claim them.

## How to Play

- 2-4 players take turns drawing lines between adjacent dots
- When a player completes the 4th side of a box, they claim it and get another turn
- Game ends when all boxes are claimed
- Player with the most claimed boxes wins
- If you complete multiple boxes in one move, you keep going

## Controls

Click or tap on the space between two dots to draw a line.

## Scoring

Each completed box scores 1 point. The player with the most boxes at the end wins.

## Game Options

- Players: 2-4 (human and/or computer)
- Grid size: 4x4 to 6x6
- Dark mode toggle

## Features

- SVG-based board rendering
- AI opponent with strategic move logic
- Undo last move
- Hover effects on available lines
- Responsive design
- Dark/light mode
