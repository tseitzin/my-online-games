# Race Game

## Overview

Multi-player arcade racing game with 4 different track types, supporting up to 4 human players and 4 AI racers simultaneously.

## How to Play

- Choose number of human players (1-4) and AI racers (0-4, max 8 total cars)
- Select track type and number of laps
- Customize car colors and numbers
- Race around the track - first to complete all laps wins
- Cars have acceleration, braking, and steering
- AI cars race at approximately 85-95% of max speed with slight variation

## Controls

- **Player 1:** W/Space (accelerate), S (brake), A/D (steer) -- OR Arrow keys
- **Player 2:** Arrow keys (Up/Down/Left/Right)
- **Player 3:** I (accelerate), K (brake), J/L (steer)
- **Player 4:** Numpad 8 (accelerate), Numpad 5 (brake), Numpad 4/6 (steer)
- **Touch:** On-screen buttons for acceleration, brake, left, right

## Scoring

Position-based finish order. Lap completion is tracked per car.

## Game Options

- Human players: 1-4
- AI racers: 0-4 (up to 8 total cars)
- Track type: Oval, Figure-8, Road Course, Speedway
- Laps: 1-20 (default 3)
- Car customization: 8 colors (red, blue, green, orange, yellow, cyan, pink, white) and car numbers

## Features

- Canvas-based rendering
- Real-time physics (acceleration, deceleration, coast, steering)
- Multi-player split keyboard controls
- Touch controls for mobile
- Countdown sequence
- HUD with positions and lap progress
- Responsive canvas
